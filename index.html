Why I love photography

Photography allows me to freeze beautiful moments in time. It helps me express creativity, preserve memories, and appreciate the beauty that surrounds us every day. I love capturing the essence of a moment and joy in it. The passion of photography is the ability to capture the moment. photography has a touch that makes it special. The touch is a mix of technical skills and artistic vision. Each technical skill is a tool that helps me capture the perfect shot. The artistic vision must be present to make the photograph truly special.

The essentials of photography

lets look at the camera and the lens.The camera is the device that captures the image. The lens is the device that focuses the light onto the sensor. The sensor is the device that converts the light into electrical signals. The lens is the cameras eye which produces the shot Just like the human eye it needs good lighting to capture shots.
